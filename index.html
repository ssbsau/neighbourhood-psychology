Our approach

Strengths first. Diagnoses second.

We don't try to "fix" the way a child thinks. Instead, we work to understand it — how they take in the world, what energises them, what overwhelms them, and where small shifts can make a big difference at home and at school.

From there, therapy and skill-building become a genuine collaboration: with the child, the family, and the people around them. The goal is confidence, regulation, and connection — in ways that feel authentic to who they are.

Access & logistics

In-person in Maroochydore. Online anywhere in Australia.

We see clients in person at our practice on Plaza Parade, and via secure Telehealth right across the country. Sessions are bookable directly online — usually within a week or two.

Medicare Mental Health Care Plans are accepted, so eligible clients receive a rebate on each session with a GP referral. We can help you understand the process if it's new to you.
