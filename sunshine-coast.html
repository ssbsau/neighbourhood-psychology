Local practice

Psychologist in Maroochydore & the Sunshine Coast

Professional psychological support for children, adolescents, adults and families — in-person at our Maroochydore practice, or via Telehealth anywhere in Australia.

Neighbourhood Psychology provides thoughtful, personalised care that is responsive to each person's stage of life, circumstances and goals. Our approach is calm, respectful and evidence-informed — creating a space where people feel genuinely heard and understood.

Psychological support across the Sunshine Coast

We work with individuals and families throughout the Sunshine Coast region, including Maroochydore and surrounding communities. Many people seek support during times of uncertainty, increased stress or developmental change, and value working with a psychologist who understands the local context and pace of life.

Appointments are available in person at our Maroochydore practice, with Telehealth also offered for those who prefer the flexibility of online sessions.

In-person psychology in Maroochydore

Being based in Maroochydore allows us to provide accessible, in-person psychology services within a welcoming and private setting. We understand the pressures that can come with balancing work, school, family life and personal wellbeing.

Our practice offers a calm environment where individuals and families can build a strong therapeutic relationship and feel comfortable engaging in the process of therapy.

Supporting children and families

Working with children and families is central to our practice. Therapy with children is approached in a gentle and age-appropriate manner, helping young people better understand their emotions, develop coping skills and build confidence.

Parents and caregivers are supported throughout the process, with guidance designed to reinforce progress in everyday situations and strengthen family relationships.

Areas of support

  • Anxiety and excessive worry
  • Emotional regulation and coping skills
  • Stress, overwhelm and burnout
  • Low mood and emotional difficulties
  • Life transitions and adjustment
  • Relationship and family challenges
  • Neurodevelopmental assessments (Autism, ADHD, learning profiles)
  • Veterans and first responders

Our vision, mission & strengths

Vision: A calmer, connected community where children, families and adults access timely, compassionate psychology and thrive throughout life together.

Mission: We provide evidence-based therapy and practical strategies, partnering with families and individuals to build resilience, emotional regulation and wellbeing.

Strengths: Flexible in-person and Telehealth care, delivered with experience, compassion and respect for each person and family.

Common questions

Do you provide psychology services across the Sunshine Coast?

Yes. We support children, adolescents, adults and families from across the Sunshine Coast region, including Maroochydore and surrounding communities.

Where are appointments held?

Appointments are available in person at our Maroochydore practice at 7-9 Plaza Parade, with Telehealth also offered for clients across the Sunshine Coast who prefer online sessions.

Do you work with local schools or GPs?

Where appropriate, we can collaborate with schools, GPs and other professionals to support coordinated care for children, adolescents and families.

Do you work with NDIS clients?

Yes. We work with self-managed and plan-managed NDIS participants.
