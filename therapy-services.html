Therapy & Assessments

From a single consultation to in-depth neurodevelopmental assessment, here's what we offer at Neighbourhood Psychology.

Whether you're looking for clarity through an assessment, ongoing therapeutic support, or somewhere to start when you're not yet sure what you need — we're glad to help you work it out. Sessions are available in person in Maroochydore and via Telehealth Australia-wide.

Therapy services

Therapy is collaborative. We use approaches that fit the person and the problem — including cognitive-behavioural therapy (CBT), acceptance and commitment therapy (ACT), exposure-based work, and play-based therapy with younger children. Above all, sessions are warm, practical, and paced to feel manageable.

Anxiety & Mood Therapies

Evidence-based therapy for anxiety, depression and mood difficulties — including panic, social anxiety, low mood, and persistent worry — in children, teens and adults.

Specialist assessments

Our assessments are designed to give a clear picture: what's going on, what's working, and what kind of support would help. The aim is practical insight you can actually use — for home, school, work or further treatment.

Autism assessments

Comprehensive autism assessment for children, teens and adults, using current best-practice tools and conducted in a neurodiversity-affirming way.

ADHD assessments

Structured assessment for attention and executive function differences. Useful for diagnosis, school/work accommodations, and treatment planning.

Fees, Medicare & how to start

Sessions are typically 50 minutes. Eligible clients can claim a Medicare rebate with a GP referral and Mental Health Care Plan. Assessment fees vary depending on the type and complexity — we'll walk you through this clearly before anything is booked.

If you're not sure where to begin, the easiest path is to book a brief initial appointment. We'll use that time to understand what you're hoping for and recommend a sensible next step.
